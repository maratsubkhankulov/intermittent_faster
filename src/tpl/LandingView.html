Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Mittent?

Choose a fasting pattern such as 16:8, 5:2, Warrior diet. Sign up for Mittent. Log your meals and track your eating pattern. Commit for a habit-forming 21 days. Once it's over - measure changes in weight, body fat, sleep quality and energy levels.

What is intermittent fasting?

Intermittent - occasional, something done periodically. Fasting - not digesting food.

Why does intermittent fasting work?

Through anecdotal, historical and scientific research it is emerging that regular fasting is a healthy habit. It can range from skipping breakfast to foregoing eating for one day out of a week. The touted benefits include fat-loss, longevity, improved recovery time from exercise, better sleep, increased energy and alertness, a simpler way of eating, greater receptivity to food. The underlying reasons for fasting's efficacy may be complex and unclear, but why wait for the scientists to catch up? try it out for yourself.
