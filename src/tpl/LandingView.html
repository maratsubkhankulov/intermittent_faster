What is intermittent fasting?

Why does intermittent fasting work?

Through anecdotal, historical and scientific research it is emerging that regular fasting is a healthy habit. This can range from skipping breakfast to foregoing eating for one day out of a week. The frequently touted health benefits include fat-loss, improved recovery time, increased energy and alertness as well as simplicity. The underlying reasons are complex and unclear.
